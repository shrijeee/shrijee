Ratan Tata

Industrialist | Philanthropist | Visionary

1937-2024

About

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist and former chairman of Tata Sons. Born on December 28, 1937, he led the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012 and again as interim chairman from October 2016 through February 2017. Under his leadership, Tata Group expanded globally and achieved major milestones like acquiring Jaguar Land Rover and Corus Steel.

Known for his humility and philanthropy, Ratan Tata has donated billions through the Tata Trusts for healthcare, education, and rural development. He has received the Padma Vibhushan and Padma Bhushan for his contributions to India.

Famous Quotes

  • "Ups and downs in life are very important to keep us going, because a straight line even in an ECG means we are not alive."
